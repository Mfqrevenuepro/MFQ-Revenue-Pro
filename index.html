Comprehensive Practice Management

We manage the full revenue cycle so your team can focus on patient care while we protect and grow your practice's financial health.

  • Advance eligibility & benefits verification
  • Rejection fixations and clean claim submission
  • Account receivables (A/R) management
  • Denial management and appeals
  • Patient billing support and statements
  • Revenue analysis and reporting
  • Daily, weekly, and monthly comprehensive reports

End-to-end credentialing and enrollment support to keep your providers active and in-network without administrative headaches.

  • Federal / Government payers
  • Commercial payers
  • Revalidations and provider updates
  • EDI enrollment and ERA / EFT setup
  • Insurance portal creations and maintenance

We streamline patient access with efficient scheduling support that keeps your calendar full and patients informed.

  • Patient scheduling and rescheduling of appointments
  • Calling patients for balance and insurance confirmation
  • Answering patient queries and reminders

Experienced medical scribes who support your physicians in real time, improving documentation quality and freeing more time for patient interaction.

  • Accurate documentation of patient encounters, medical history, physical exams, assessments, and treatment plans
  • Transcription of verbal notes from healthcare providers into EHRs or paper charts
  • Entry of lab tests, radiology orders, and prescriptions into the EHR system
  • Ensuring completeness and accuracy of patient records
